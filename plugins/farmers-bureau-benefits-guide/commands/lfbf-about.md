# About Louisiana Farm Bureau — Working for All Louisiana Citizens

Louisiana Farm Bureau Federation (LFBF) is more than a membership organization. It is Louisiana's largest general farm organization and one of the state's most consequential voices for agricultural policy, rural investment, environmental stewardship, and education — work that touches every Louisiana citizen whether they are a farmer or not.
